Brief

Meetings when you can, where you can.

Brief is an app to showcase your available time slots in your schedule. You fill in your location and time, and then you are matched with people that also are up for a brief moment.

The matching

The matching works so that you choose which person you want to meet, and they have to want to choose you.

The midpoint recommendation

The midpoint of your locations is then calculated and a coffee shop at that location is then presented to you through foursquare.

Target group

People with busy schedules, but who might have that extra slot where they would like to meet up with professional people. By using only LinkedIn as a login, we want to keep it professional.

Where will sustainability come from?

For most of human history, collaboration and sustainability were key ingredients for a good life. People worked together to provide their communities with necessities in a way that benefited everyone. With the advent of industrilization, priorities changed and wealth accumulated. With the advent of consumer technology, the customers gained power, leveraging competition in the market to get access to better products to a lower price.

With the advent of the information age, we are shifting our priorities. We have realized that we all need to add to the collective good, not take from it. But after living with the benefits of the free market for 200 years, we as consumers won't settle for anything less than what we are used to.

The future marketplace will be dominated by the products that produce win-win-win; the creator gets their cut, the customer gets their needs satisfied, and the all of society benefits â€“ directly. Making stuff that is only good for the buyer and the seller won't cut it anymore. Win-win-win will be the hidden successfactor of the great products of the comming century.
